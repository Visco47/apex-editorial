FAQ

Common questions.

How does Cadence integrate with our existing tools?

+

Cadence connects via official APIs to Jira, Linear, GitHub, GitLab, Notion, Slack, and more. Setup takes under 5 minutes per tool. We read data — we never modify your existing workflows or create issues on your behalf unless you configure autopilot rules.

What is "Predictive Routing" exactly?

+

Our ML models analyze your team's historical delivery patterns — who works on what, how long stages take, and where handoffs stall. When it detects a likely bottleneck forming, it suggests or automatically reroutes work to available capacity. Think of it as a traffic system for your sprints.

Is my data secure?

+

Yes. All data is encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3). Enterprise plans offer dedicated instances in your preferred cloud region. We are SOC 2 Type II certified and GDPR compliant. We never train models on your data.

Can I try Cadence before committing?

+

Absolutely. The Starter plan is free forever for teams up to 10 people. The Pro plan includes a 14-day free trial — no credit card required. You'll see value within the first sprint.

How long does onboarding take?

+

Most teams are fully onboarded within a day. Connect your tools (5 min each), invite your team, and Cadence begins learning immediately. Predictive features become accurate after about 2 sprints of data.
